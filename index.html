FoxGlove is an independent film collective made up of a group of friends aiming to share their vision through the art of filmmaking. We produce original and independent films, and wish to share them with the world.

PROJECTS

The Third Rail Trailer

Our upcoming feature film. A dark action comedy about one man's goal of pursuing the leader of a dangerous organization of assassins. This film is special to us, as it was made even though we now live in different countries, making planning and shooting difficult. the making of this film shows how well we can work in unison and collaborate despite tight schedules, ridiculous wake-up times and actors and crew living great distances apart. This film also marks a step forward in terms of effects. We always want to push for trying new things, by either incoorporating practical or 3D effects or new locations. With this project we invested time into experimental lighting, colour grading, practical effects and VFX. As of now, this is the most ambitious projects that we've taken on, the result of which we are proud to present.
